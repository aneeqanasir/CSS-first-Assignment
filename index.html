Three Famous Cities Of Pakistan

KARACHI
Karachi is Pakistan's largest city, main seaport, and premier financial and industrial hub. Located on the Arabian Sea, it is the capital of Sindh. Home to a diverse, multicultural population of over 20 million, the megacity generates about 25% of Pakistan's GDP and handles 95% of its foreign trade

Lahore
Lahore is Pakistan’s second-largest city and the historic, cultural capital of the Punjab province. With a population exceeding 14 million, it is celebrated as the "City of Gardens" and is renowned for its UNESCO World Heritage sites, rich literary traditions, and vibrant, legendary street food scene

Islamabad
Islamabad, the capital of Pakistan, is a meticulously planned, serene, and green metropolis nestled against the lush Margalla Hills. It is uniquely defined by its grid-based sector layout, wide tree-lined avenues, and iconic landmarks like the majestic Faisal Mosque and the Pakistan Monument. The city offers a highly peaceful environment and excellent quality of life.
